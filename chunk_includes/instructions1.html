Instructions:

During this experiment, you will be presented with mini-biographies about fictional cultural figures.

Before each new mini-biography, you will see an ellipsis ( ... ). Press the spacebar when you're ready to continue.

You will first be presented with a sentence about the cultural figure (e.g., George Carlon was an American comedian. He died in California.). When you have finished reading this first sentence, press the spacebar.

You will then be presented with another sentence in chunks, describing an accomplishment of the same cultural figure. When you have read the first chunk (e.g., He ) press the spacebar and the next chunk will appear (e.g., recorded ) . After you have read this chunk, press the spacebar and the next chunk will appear (e.g., several stand-up records), and so on. Please try to read at a natural speed.

After this 'chunked' sentence, you will be presented with a “rating scale” from 1 (definitely wrong) to 7 (perfectly fine). Please rate the naturalness of the 'chunked' sentence given the context of the first sentence using the mouse.

You will have a practice round to get familiarised with the procedure.

At the end of the experiment, you will be presented with a link. You must click on this link to receive your payment!

Click 'Continue' to start the practice round.
